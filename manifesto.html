This (student-run) website is a record of the work of MArch students from Central Saint Martins in the summer of 2020.

It brings together a series of calls to action, under the banner of Speaking to Power, a collection of radical broadcasts live streamed on the 23/09/20 and archived on the site. It acts as an archive for past and future work, a place to record the agitation.
