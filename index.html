This (student-run) website is a record of the work of MArch students from Central Saint Martins in the summer of 2020.

It's centred on a rewriting of the Spatial Practices manifesto, making fluid a static document. We address the emergence of new ways of building, living and organising in response to these needs.

On the 23/09/20 it will transform into a television and radio station to broadcast a number of messages from the course, which will then be archived on the site along with other work.
