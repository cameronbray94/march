We invite you to join us for an afternoon of programming to celebrate the graduating students from Central Saint Martins’ Masters in Architecture.

It’s been a difficult year to wrap up our studies, but we have risen to the challenge, asking big and bold questions about the future of the built environment.

We present a number of short films, podcasts and performances live and direct from MArch HQ in Kings Cross, London.

Please join us on the channels to the left for a live-stream of the event, and in the chatroom for questions.
